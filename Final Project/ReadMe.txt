Thank you for taking the time for playing my games and checking out my website.

Batwing Adventures is a game where you click the buttons on the screen to control the Batwing and avoid obstacles.
Try to see how long you can survive and create your highest score!

A Day in the Mushroom Kingdom is a basic game where you click and drag the Mushroom Kingdom Characters around.

Mind Games is a puzzle game, where you point and click the blank tiles and the numbers around the blank tiles,
in order for you to organize the numbers from 1-15.
